What is Expense Tracker?

The Expense Tracker is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you manage your personal finances. Whether you're budgeting, saving for a specific goal, or just tracking your daily expenses, this app has you covered.

Key Features

- Track your expenses by category.
- Set monthly budgets and financial goals.
- Generate detailed spending reports.
- Customize expense categories to suit your needs.

Why Use It?

This tracker provides you with the insights you need to make smarter financial decisions. Take control of your finances today!
